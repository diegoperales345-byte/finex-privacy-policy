Privacy Policy – Finanzas Personales (Finex)

Data Collection and Use

We collect information such as your name, email, profile picture, and transactions to improve your experience within the app. Your profile picture may be obtained from your email, or you can change it within the app.

Storage and Privacy

All data and photos are stored locally on your device only and are not shared with third parties. The app may request permission to send notifications, but all information related to these permissions is handled locally.

User Control

You can change your personal data at any time within the app. The option to delete your data will be available in future updates.

Security

All data is stored locally on your device and is not sent to external servers, ensuring your information remains secure and private.

Use of Data

Data is used only for app functions, such as:

  • Displaying your profile and personal transactions.
  • Enabling app features related to your finances.
  • Sending local reminders or alerts within the app.

Contact

For questions or concerns about your information, you can contact us via the app’s support section.
